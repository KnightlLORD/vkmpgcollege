Welcome To Vindhya Kanya P. G. College

Vindhya Kanya Mahavidyalay was established in 2006.The College offers career in study through various courses. Dedicated teachers, well equipped Library, and other infrastructures conducive for learning and development are available.

The college offers separate valuable facilities to the students and staff for their progression and career building.The college aims to provide a complete, meaningful and relevant education to women so that they are intellectually well-trained, morally upright, socially aware and spiritually inspired.

It would be pertinent to note that despite coming out from a traditionally prosperous family, every far thing, spent in opening the schools infrastructures investments, was self earned, not even borrowed either from the family wealth or from any friend or well-wishers.

M.COM

B.COM

Bachelor of commerce (b.com) is an undergraduate traditional cum professional course which make a student competent.

B.ED

BTC

M.A

Master of Arts is a postgraduate programme in arts. MA can be pursued by students after completion of their bachelor's degree.

B.Sc

B.Sc. (Bachelor of Science) is an undergraduate course affiliated to MGKVP Varanasi. It is of three year duration course.
